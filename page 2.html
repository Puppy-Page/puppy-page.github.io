Dangers of Stress

How to find one

Stress has many symptoms incuding headaches, upset stomach or just tired all the time.

What can cause it?

A number of events like work and relationships. Anything that makes you feel not yourself.

What can stress do?

having a little bit of stress is fine from time-to-time. Too much of it can cause high blood pressure, heart disease, diabetes, and obesity. Thats just all the physical probelms a person can have, there is all sorts of mental problems like depression, forgetfullness, headaches, lack of energy and many more.

Are there differents types of stress?

Yes there is. There is acute stress and there is chronic stress. Acute stress is short term and last long enough to get your task down like ski trip down a steep slope. This is the healthy stress, but any longer can lead to chronic stress. Chronic stress is when you get use to stress, always on high-alert and active and many times, you don't see the problem till its too late. It will lead to many problems if it is not taken care of.
