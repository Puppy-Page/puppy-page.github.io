How to: Stress Relief

Talk about it

Many people forget to talk about the problems they face, keeping it bottled up inside, making stress.

Exercise

Letting the fat build up in your body is no sure way to feel better anytime soon. Burn it away, along with the stress.

Drink healthy

Put down that third cup of coffee and try some tea once in a while

Write

If there are things you can't speak to anyone about, write it down

Laugh

A short laugh goes a long way
